## The decision

Because it's a time tracker.

Not a social network.

Not a workspace platform.

Not another service that needs my email address.

I built Flowtime because I wanted something simple.

Open the app.

Start the timer.

Track my work.

That's it.

Accounts solve some problems.

They also create new ones.

For Flowtime, the tradeoff isn't worth it.

The app should work the moment you install it.

No sign-up.

No login.

No password reset.

Just time tracking.
