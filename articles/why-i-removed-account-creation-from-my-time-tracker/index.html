Why Flowtime Will Never Require an Account

A short product decision note on why Flowtime stays account-free, simple, and ready to use immediately.

Ava Thalheim Jun 6, 2026 1 min read
FlowtimeProduct DesignLessons Learned

Contents

The decision

The decision

Because it's a time tracker.

Not a social network.

Not a workspace platform.

Not another service that needs my email address.

I built Flowtime because I wanted something simple.

Open the app.

Start the timer.

Track my work.

That's it.

Accounts solve some problems.

They also create new ones.

For Flowtime, the tradeoff isn't worth it.

The app should work the moment you install it.

No sign-up.

No login.

No password reset.

Just time tracking.
