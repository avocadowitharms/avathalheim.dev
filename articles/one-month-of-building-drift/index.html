Month one

The first month of Drift has mostly been about finding the product's center.

I know I want a workspace around repositories. The question is how much belongs in the first version and how much should wait until the product has earned more complexity.

What moved forward

  • Clearer positioning around repository-first project management
  • Early thinking around tasks, docs, and project context
  • Better separation between product ideas and implementation details
  • A growing list of things not to build yet

What I am watching

The biggest risk is turning Drift into another heavy project management tool with a developer-themed wrapper.

The goal is the opposite: less overhead, more context, and a product that helps me resume real work faster.

That is the thread I want to keep following.
